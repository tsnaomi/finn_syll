FinnSyll

Syllabifying Finnish in Python.

Primary goals

  • Identifying unmarked compound boundaries
  • Exploring vowel (T4) variation using Prosodic

Secondary goals

  • Exploring variation in Finnish consonant clusters

Open questions

  • Is /au/ always ambiguous in unstressed syllables? E.g., rakkauden.
  • Can we complicate T4 to only care about onsets and not codas?
  • Can we use back/front vowel harmony to detect compound boundaries?

Q & A

  • Does the syllabifier always join long vowels? Yes, this rule is encompassed in T6.
